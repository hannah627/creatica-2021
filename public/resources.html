Resources

Centralizing mental health recourses of employees outside of the workplace.

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health, consider reaching out to one (or more) of the resources, facilities, or helplines below.

If you or someone you know is in crisis, call the Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-TALK (8255). If it's an emergency, call 911 or your country's respective emergency services line.
